Meatballs


Description

Meatballs is a classic dish that tastes good!

Ingredients

  • Ground meat
  • Garlic
  • Parsley
  • Black pepper
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Italian bread
  • Water
  • Olive oil
  1. Combine the first six ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the bread crumbs and slowly add the water.
  3. Shape the mixture into meatballs.
  4. Fry the meatballs until they're all brown and crisp.
